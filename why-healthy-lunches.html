Why Healthy Lunches Matter

A healthy lunch helps teenagers stay energised, focused, and ready for the rest of the school day.

Benefits of a balanced lunch

  • Supports energy throughout the day.
  • Helps concentration in class.
  • Provides nutrients for growth and development.
  • Reduces the urge to snack on unhealthy food later.
