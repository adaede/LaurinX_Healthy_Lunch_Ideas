Snacks and Drinks

A good lunch should include healthy snacks and drinks to support energy and hydration.

Healthy Snacks

Hydration

Water is the best drink to keep the body hydrated during the school day.

Balanced Choices

Combine protein, fruit and grains to stay full for longer.
